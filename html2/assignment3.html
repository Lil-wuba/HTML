India is a large and diverse country located in South Asia. It is known for its rich history, cultural heritage,
    and traditions. India is the world's most populous country and is home to people of different religions, languages,
    and customs. The capital city of India is New Delhi, while Mumbai is its financial hub. India is famous for its historical
    monuments, such as the Taj Mahal, colorful festivals, delicious cuisine, and contributions to science and technology.
    Agriculture, industry, and information technology play important roles in the country's economy. India is also the
     world's largest democracy and continues to grow as a major global power.
